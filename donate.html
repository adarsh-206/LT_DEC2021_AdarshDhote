Why donate us ?

Inner Satisfaction

Human beings are social people. They take care of each other and feel good when someone extends their help to them in the time of need.

So, when they find someone who they care about, in need of help, they gladly help them, without expecting any kind of a favour in return.

Trust Factor

In childhood, we had learned a proverb, “Charity begins at home”. This is a particularly important lesson in everyone’s life.

People are more eager to extend their helping hand to a local organization whose work they can easily monitor on a personal level.

Charity begins at home

Face value is an especially important thing in this modern world. People tend to trust people who have a clean image and are publicly acceptable.

For example, if Shah Rukh Khan endorses an NGO, it is sure to get more help than the NGO he does not endorse.

Be a Volunteer

Volunteering helps others, but it can also help you, too. If you're upset about something that's happened — like a hurricane or other disaster — doing something about it can be a great way to cope with your feelings.
